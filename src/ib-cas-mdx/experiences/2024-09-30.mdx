# Introduction to The Activity

To execute the following 30-minute workout plan around 5 times a week, building body strength at home with little equipment required.

# CAS Stages

## Investigation

Look through introductory calisthenics videos on YouTube for guidance.

## Preparation

Find suitable spaces and any necessary equipment.

## Action

**Warm-up (4 minutes)**

- Jumping jacks: 1 minute
- High knees: 1 minute
- Bodyweight squats: 1 minute
- Arm circles: 1 minute (30 seconds in each direction)

**Main Workout (20 minutes)**

- Bodyweight Squats (3 sets x 15 reps)
- Wall Push-Ups (3 sets x 12-15 reps)
- Bodyweight Rows (3 sets x 10-12 reps)
- Reverse Lunges (2 sets x 12 reps per leg)
- Plank with Shoulder Taps (2 sets x 45-60 seconds):

**Cool Down (6 minutes)**

- Downward Dog: 1 minute
- Cobra Stretch: 1 minute
- Child's Pose: 1 minute
- Hip Flexor Stretch: 1 minute per leg
- Shoulder Stretch: 1 minute
- Neck Stretch: 1 minute
